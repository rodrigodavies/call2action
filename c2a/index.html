BoxesBlocks
To begin, drag boxes from the toolkit onto the canvas. To link two elements, drag from the yellow exit node of one box to the blue entry node of another. To delete a link, double click it. To delete a box, drag it onto the delete button at the bottom right.
Blocks are commonly used sequences of boxes. You can upload your own audio files, but you can't edit the flow of a block. If you need a different flow, use boxes.
